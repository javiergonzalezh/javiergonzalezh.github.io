I am research associate in the Machine Learning group of the University of Sheffield working with Neil Lawrence. My reseach focuses on different Statistical and Machine Learning aspects of Data Science, specially on Bayesian Optimization and other Reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces/Gaussian processes based approaches. I am interested in visualization, dynamical modeling and networks reconstruction problems and their applications in Systems and Synthetic Biology.

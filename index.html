I am research associate in the Machine Learning group of the University of Sheffield working with Neil Lawrence. My reseach focuses on Machine Learning aspects of Data Science, specially on Bayesian Optimization and other Reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces/Gaussian processes based approaches. I am also interested in the the application of new Machine Learning techniques in Systems and Synthetic Biology.

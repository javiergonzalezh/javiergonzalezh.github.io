I am research associate in the Machine Learning group of the University of Sheffield. I work on the Statistical and Machine Learning aspects of Data Science, specially on Bayesian Optimization and other Reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces/Gaussian processes based approaches. I am interested in visualization, dynamical modeling and networks reconstruction problems and their applications in Systems and Synthetic Biology.
